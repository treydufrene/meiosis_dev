Specification \textbf{1.2.a} states: \textbf{The system shall consist of six rotational joints connected by four links. The last three joints will create a spherical wrist.} For this specification to be met, the robot must have six rotational joints and 4 links. The last three joints must create a spherical wrist where the axis of revolution for the last three joints are intersecting with one another. To perform a test to check for six rotational joints, SolidWorks is required. A render of the manipulator with labeled joints can be seen in Figure 1.
